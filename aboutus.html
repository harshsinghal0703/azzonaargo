About Us

Azzona Agro is operating since 2024 in B2B & FMCG industry with an objective to cover end to end value chain & increase farmers yearly income with establishing agro food processing unites. the company has been potentially expanding in empowering women, tribes, and small farmers across various regions of India.

Azzona aims to establish as a affordable quality FMCG product brand by leveraging Tech, proficient team, best industry practices and etc.. We work closely with stakeholders to implement sustainable practices and promote an ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) model. Our focus is on enhancing the earning capacity and livelihoods of each stakeholder through continuous improvement initiatives.

Our Journey and Goals

Our Begning

Founded in 2024, Azzona Agro has grown to become a leading player in the agri-processing and agri-trading industry. Our team comprises experts with extensive experience in agriculture, engineering, and market analysis. We bring together a wealth of knowledge and a shared commitment to advancing agricultural practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers, producers, and traders by providing top-notch agri-processing technologies and facilitating seamless trade opportunities. We aim to enhance the quality of agricultural products, optimize processing methods, and connect stakeholders across the agricultural sector.
