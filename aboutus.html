About Us
Azzona Agro is operating since 2024 in B2B & FMCG industry with an objective to cover end to end value chain & increase farmers yearly income with establishing agro food processing unites. The company has been potentially expanding in empowering women, tribes, and small farmers across various regions of India.
Azzona aims to establish as aaffordable quality FMCG product brand by leveraging Tech, proficient team, best industry practices and etc.. We work closely with stakeholders to implement sustainable practices and promote an ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) model. Our focus is on enhancing the earning capacity and livelihoods of each stakeholder through continuous improvement initiatives
